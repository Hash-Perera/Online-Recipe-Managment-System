Italian Fried Rice
Fried rice is really versatile, so this is the time that you can clean out your fridge with any leftovers you might have!

Italian Fried Rice is our twist on a traditional fried rice recipe, done Italian style! An easy dinner recipe that the whole family will love!
Ingredients
White Rice
Italian Sausages
Bellpeppers
Onions
Mozzarella Cheese
Tomato
Preparation
  1. Leftover white rice, or cooked and cooled rice.
  2. Italian Sausage, spicy or mild.
  3. Green and red bell peppers.
  4. Mozzarella cheese balls.
  5. Sun dried tomatoes.
  6. Fried rice should be cooked quickly at a high temperature to get a nice sear on the ingredients.
  7. chances are your rice will burn or get overcooked.
